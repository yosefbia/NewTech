MINECRAFT 2D

Harvest a hand-built 2D world block by block, fill your pack and build it back your way. Every texture below is pure CSS — the same tiles you will meet in the game.

▶ Play the classic world Roll a random world ↓

How to play

1

Pick a tool. Click it in the toolbar or press 1–4. The selected tool lights up green.

2

Harvest blocks. Click a block that matches your tool and it pops out of the world. Wrong tool? The block shakes it off.

3

Fill your pack. Every harvested block lands in your pack, stacked with a count.

4

Build. Click a block in your pack, then click any empty spot — sky or cave — to place it.

5

Start over. Reset restores the original world. New world rolls a random one.

Roll your own world
